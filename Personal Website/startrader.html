Star Trader

Trading on a galactic scale.

Generation
Planets are randomly generated in a grid. Each planet gets a technology level ranging from pre-historic to futuristic. This technology level affects what goods the planet will have available to buy, and what goods the planet will be interested in purchasing.
Encounters
While traveling the player has a chance to trigger several different encounters. These encounters include space polics, space pirates, and other space traders. Each of these encounters provides the player with different options that have different probabilities of success. For example, when encountering a pirate, the player can choose to run away and not lose any cargo, but they may fail and lose even more cargo.
Goals
Once the player has done a lot of trading and become very wealthy, there are a few endgame goals for the player to accomplish. The player can purchase planets individually, and eventually the player can own the entire galaxy.
